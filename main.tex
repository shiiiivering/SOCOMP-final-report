\noindent\begin{CJK*}{GBK}{hei}摘\quad 要\quad \end{CJK*} \begin{CJK*}{GBK}{song}
随着互联网技术的发展和普及，人们正在网络上生产越来越大量的数据，向用户推荐符合其预期和需求的信息已经成为互联网信息服务商的重要任务。推荐系统在这项工作中起到了很重要的作用，它能够依据用户过去的选择或者潜在的兴趣向用户推荐其可能感兴趣的信息。由于用户的社会关系能在推荐的过程中产生一定的作用，社交推荐正在吸引越来越多的研究者的注意。在这篇综述中，我们首先会介绍传统的推荐算法并介绍其局限性，随后我们以一些具有代表性的算法为例介绍社交推荐的各类模型和效果，并且和传统推荐算法进行比较。我们将社交推荐算法大致分为基于内存的社交推荐、基于模型的社交推荐两类分别介绍。此外，随着计算机技术和GPU等硬件的兴起，深度学习和神经网络等技术兴起，研究者们也将这些技术应用于社交推荐系统中，通过训练和学习来提取更多的特征以求更好的推荐效果和精确度以及计算效率。我们将挑选一些具有代表性的网络进行介绍。最后我们综合社交推荐系统的发展过程，总结推荐算法的进步和现存的问题，并给出将来可能的发展建议。

{\bf Abstract}\quad \begin{CJK*}{GBK}{hei}With the development and popularization of Internet technology, people are producing more and more large amounts of data on the Internet, and recommending information that meets their expectations and needs to users has become an important task for Internet information service providers. The recommendation system plays a very important role in this work. It can recommend information that may be of interest to users based on their past choices or potential interests. Because the user's social relationship can play a certain role in the recommendation process, social recommendation is attracting more and more researchers' attention. In this review, we will first introduce traditional recommendation algorithms and introduce their limitations, and then we will introduce various models and effects of social recommendation using some representative algorithms as examples, and compare them with traditional recommendation algorithms. We roughly divide the social recommendation algorithm into memory-based social recommendation and model-based social recommendation. In addition, with the rise of computer technology and hardware such as GPUs, and the rise of technologies such as deep learning and neural networks, researchers have also applied these technologies to social recommendation systems to extract more features through training and learning for better The recommended effect and accuracy and calculation efficiency. We will select some representative networks for introduction. Finally, we integrate the development process of the social recommendation system, summarize the progress of the recommendation algorithm and existing problems, and give possible future development suggestions.\end{CJK*}
